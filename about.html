Welcome

To use this website, first press the two download buttons, then the two black canvases will show the 3D proteins base on the files download from http://www.pdb.org If we move the mouse over the amino acid sequence, the proteins will show the corresponding amino acid molecules on the 3D models. We also can use our mouse to move, rotate and scale the proteins on the 3D canvas.

After press both download buttons, we can click the "Align Proteins". Then the page shows aligned protein sequences.

Thanks to the open source project GLmol Molecular Viewer http://webglmol.sourceforge.jp/index-en.html and the multiple protein structure alignment program Matt( M. Menke, B. Berger, L. Cowen, "Matt: Local Flexibility Aids Protein Multiple Structure Alignment", PLOS Computational Biology, Vol. 4, No 1., 2008. ).
